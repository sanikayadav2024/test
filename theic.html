Technical Specification

  • CAS NO: 839-90-7
  • Molecular Weight: 261.2

Applications

Used in resin manufacturing for various coatings:

  • Electrical Insulating Varnishes
  • Magnet Wire Channels
  • Alkyds
  • Unsaturated Polyesters
  • Heat Resistant Lubricating Oils
Packing

25 Kgs Bags

Storage

Hygroscopic. Keep tightly sealed to avoid crystal formation. Store in a protected area.

Safety

Not hazardous. Combustible and explosive as dust form.

Solubility

Soluble in water, THF, and lower alcohols. Insoluble in common organic solvents.
